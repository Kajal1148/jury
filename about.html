About Us
Affiliation
The school is affiliated with Central Board of Secondary Education with Affiliation Number 520004and school number is 40058.
The school got its affiliation renewed for a term of five years from March 2019 to Feb. 2024.




Houses
The School is grouped under four Houses, namely : Indra, Soma, Surya and Varuna. Each House is directly under the charge of House Master/Mistress assisted by the House Tutors.The House Master/Mistress acts as the local guardian of child in his/her House.
True to its Public School nature, the School accepts the importance of the prefectural system. Thus the House Captain, Senior and Junior House perfects actively assist the House Master in efficient running of the House. The House appointments are expected to oversee and carry out the routine administration in the House. The School considers all prefects as important functionaries in the Houses.
All School Competitions in every discipline are organised as Inter-House Activities. This brings in the spirit of healthy competition so vital to spot talents and excellence. There are individual and team trophies for every such competition.
